# rewind
productivity, to do list, stay on top of things

rewind is a journaling and storytelling app that helps users capture and visualize their day as a shareable “day in the life” experience. Users log activities, moods, and places, and the app transforms that data into an interactive timeline, visual recap, and identity-based daily story. It will also act as a way for users to hold themselves accountable for their productivity throughout months and years. Will have a spotify wrapped styled ending as well to show overall progress etc. include recording clips if user wants to make it more personal. ROMANTICIZE THEIR LIFE

how to keep it engaging? beli style engagement, somehow make it addicting (focus on the romanticization aspect)
    - quicker logging
    - daily recaps
    - identity insights
